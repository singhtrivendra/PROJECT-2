Our clients

Farmers

The Platform serves as a valuable resource for farmers seeking to join FPOs or learn more about active Producer Organizations in their vicinity. By providing easy access to relevant information, the platform empowers farmers to make informed decisions and engage with organizations that best suit their needs.

Businessman

The FPO Platform offers businesses and entrepreneurs an opportunity to identify new sales channels and engage directly with FPOs. By leveraging the platform, businesses can expand their reach, connect with their target audience, and provide valuable services to FPOs and their members.

Government

Equipped with accurate and up-to-date data, the FPO Platform enables government agencies and policymakers to make informed decisions to promote and support FPOs effectively. The platform's comprehensive data allows for the development and implementation of targeted policies that can increase the success of FPOs throughout India.

FPOs

When FPOs register on the Platform, they receive verified tags that enhance their visibility among stakeholders within the FPO ecosystem. This increased visibility enables FPOs to connect with potential buyers, service providers, and other FPOs, fostering collaboration and facilitating the sale and purchase of agricultural commodities.

Researchers

The FPO Platform is an invaluable resource for researchers investigating the FPO landscape in India. Providing access to a rich database of aggregated information, the platform allows researchers to gather insights, analyze trends, and identify areas for further study in FPO-related research. By facilitating evidence-based research, the platform helps to inform policies and strategies aimed at promoting the growth and success of FPOs across the country.

Traders

FPO increases the liquidity of the company's shares by increasing the number of shares available in the market. This makes it easier for investors to buy and sell shares in the company. Diversification: An FPO allows companies to diversify its investors base as new investors buy their shares.
